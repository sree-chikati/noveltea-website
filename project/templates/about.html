About Us

Hellen Keller once said:

"Alone, we can do so littel; together we can do so much."

This quote couldn't be more applicable to the year 2020. We had individually faced numerous challanges; yet, coming together always made those challanges seem less daunting.

Similarly for me, building a community of reader helped me face some of my own problems. While a part of this community I had realized a problem we were all facing as avid book readers: we had a place to find books (ex. Google Books) and a seperate place to discuss them (ex. Discord). However, we did not have a platform there we could do both.

This is why Noveltea was created: to bring together readers to one place in which they can read their favorite books, join fandoms, and even share their expereince with the community.
